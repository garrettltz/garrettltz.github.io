Play as a mobile smoothie cafe, careening down the scenic Root 66 delivering your root vegetable smoothies to thirsty customers! CONTROLS: WSAD to drive (W to accelerate, S to brake) Mouse -- Click and drag ingredients into the blender. Click the blender to blend. Put in the right ingredients (order doesn't matter). Once you get your smoothie, chuck it out the window when the car on the ticket is next to you! But don't miss, or you'll be penalized for littering!
