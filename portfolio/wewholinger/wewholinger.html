We Who Linger was the senior capstone where I was the level designer. The player is a dead spirit that has come back to life and tries to protect the village. Explore the world that is filled with mysterious items that were left there from the long past. Built with the Unity Game Engine

We Who Linger sees the player controlling a powerful guardian spirit who alternates between defending the village and exploring the surrounding world for upgrades and resources. Fast-paced, acrobatic combat featuring supernatural abilities is juxtaposed against the quiet melancholy of exploring the nearby ruins for artifacts and memories of an earlier age. These resources help the procedurally generated villagers, who dynamically grow, develop, fight, and even die alongside you as the game progresses towards the final battle for the villageâ€™s survival.
