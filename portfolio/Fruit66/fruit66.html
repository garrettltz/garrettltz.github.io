Play as a mobile smoothie cafe, careening down the scenic Fruit 66 delivering your fruit smoothies to thirsty customers! CONTROLS: WSAD to drive (W to accelerate, S to brake) Mouse -- Click and drag ingredients into the blender. Click the blender to blend. Put in the right ingredients (order doesn't matter). Once you get your smoothie, throw the smoothie to the correct car on the ticket! But don't miss, or you'll be penalized for littering!
