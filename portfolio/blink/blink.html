Blink was a game I created that was based on the Doctor Who weeping angels where the monster of the game can only move if the player isn’t looking at it, The player needs to figure out how to leave the maze and escape before the monster kills them

Enemy Tracking Player

Using raycasts the enemy can track the player no matter where they go once the monster initially sees them

Player Tracking Enemy

Also using raycasts we can stop the enemy from moving when the player sees them, however we need to be able to move around with out having the player die. So we add four raycasts so that if the player see a part of the enemy then the enemy can’t move
